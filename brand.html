Personal Brand

Jordan Foltz

Data Analyst · Storyteller · Problem Solver

I turn raw data into clear decisions. Here's the "why" behind the work — what drives me, where I'm headed, and what I believe about doing analytics right.

Mission

To find signals of truth hidden in data and translate them into insights that drive better decisions. Supporting the organizations I serve, the teams I work alongside, and the problems worth solving. I believe data is only as powerful as the clarity with which it's communicated, and I'm committed to bridging the gap between raw information and meaningful action.

Vision

To grow into a data professional who doesn't just analyze but leads. My vision is to apply data science to the most challenging problems an organization faces, shaping strategic direction and contributing to long-term, sustainable growth. I want to build a career where my work doesn't end at a dashboard but informs the room.

Values

Truth & Trustworthiness

Every analysis I produce is only as valuable as the confidence people can place in it. I hold myself to rigorous standards of accuracy, transparency, and intellectual honesty, because decisions built on flawed data carry real consequences.

Curiosity

I don't stop at surface answers. I ask why, dig deeper, and follow the data wherever it leads, even when findings are inconvenient or unexpected. Curiosity is what separates analysis from discovery.

Growth

Learning is a permanent part of how I operate, not a phase I passed through. Whether it's a new certification, a new language, or a new domain, I'm always building toward a more capable version of myself.

Efficiency

Good work shouldn't require unnecessary friction. I look for ways to streamline, automate, and simplify — not to cut corners, but to free up time and energy for work that actually matters.

Excellence

I take pride in the quality of what I put out. From documentation to design to the logic behind a formula, details matter. If my name is on it, it should be worth standing behind.

Innovation

I bring an entrepreneurial lens to analytical work. I'm not just looking for what's been done before but for a smarter approach, a better tool, a solution that didn't exist yet.
